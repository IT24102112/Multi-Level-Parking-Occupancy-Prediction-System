About KMC Smart Car Park

A next-generation AI and IoT powered parking system for the historic city of Kandy

🚀 System Features

📡

IoT Sensor Network

Ultrasonic sensors at every entrance and exit provide second-by-second occupancy updates across all 4 levels.

🤖

AI-Powered Predictions

Two AI models — one for seasonal trends, one for real-time patterns — predict occupancy 15 minutes ahead.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Detailed occupancy reports with trends, peak hours and level comparisons to support informed decision-making.

🏛

Multi-Role Access

Separate portals for KMC admins, IT division staff and parking users — each with tailored features and access levels.

🅿 About the Car Park

The Kandy Municipal Council Car Park is a multi-level facility in the heart of Kandy, serving thousands of visitors to Sri Lanka's UNESCO World Heritage city daily. With 4 levels and 300 slots per level, this AI-powered system ensures you can find available parking quickly and efficiently.
